11Golden Rules — Non-Negotiable
1
Consistency > Optimization
A predictable system beats a perfect-but-unreliable one. Run on schedule every time.
2
Time > Perfect Load
Dispatch on time even if vehicle isn't at 90% capacity. Time is the priority.
3
Flow > Individual Order
The route serves the pool, not one customer's preference. System logic wins.
4
System Decides, Not User
Zone, vehicle, and time are assigned by the engine. Customer chooses time vs cost only.
5
Start Small, Scale After
Validate 3 orders per drive before expanding coverage to new zones.
6
Customer: Time vs Cost Only
Express (fast, expensive) or Smart (wait, cheaper). Not vehicle type, not zone.
