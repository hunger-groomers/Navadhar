The System Formula
How the routing formula works
Every pickup and delivery follows Haversine distance from Ghanta Ghar. No guesswork — pure geometry and flow logic.
01 — Zone Detection
Geographic Classification
Every location classified into N/E/S/W by comparing absolute lat/lng delta from city center.
|ΔLat| > |ΔLng| → N or S
02 — Distance
Haversine Formula
Great-circle distance in meters from each business to city center. Accounts for Earth's curvature.
d = 2R · arctan(√a / √(1−a))
03 — Pickup Order
Center → Outward
Stops sorted ascending by distance. Vehicle starts at center and collects outward — nearest first.
Route: Center → d₁ → d₂ → dₙ
04 — Delivery Order
Outward → Inward
Delivery is the exact reverse — start at farthest business and work back toward city center.
Route: dₙ → d₂ → d₁ → Center
05 — Zone Isolation
No Cross-Zone Routes
Each zone operates independently. North vehicle handles only North stops. No cross-zone zig-zag allowed.
∀ stop ∈ Zone Z → vehicles[Z]
06 — Geo-Tag
Tap-to-Register
Businesses tap their exact location on a live map. GPS coordinates auto-assign to the correct zone.
zone = f(lat, lng, CENTER)
